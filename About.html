About me

William Ware is a musician based out of Richmond, Virginia who specializes in the creation of instrumental and ambient music. With nearly a millions streams on Spotify alone, he creates under many names in order to delineate his expansion into multiple genres. His musical journey started as early as middle school, where he started creating music for piano and string orchestra. Over time he expanded out from the classical world into Lofi and more ambient tracks.

William Joe Ware is the oldest and most prominent name for the artist. Originally as he was a classical artist, it was more customary to use one's proper name or a pen name. Presently, this still functions as the sole production name he uses.

He presently plans its discontinuation in favor of: Harmonix, Cyaniros, Rythmis, and Avery Winters. This is in order to allow him to better broaden his artistic horizons without having to sacrifice resources from one productive genre to the next.

Rythmis and Cyaniros are the names given to William's adventures into Lo-Fi and Chillhop as it is a genre that he greatly enjoys (despite his middling talent for it at the moment)

Avery Winters is the pen name under which William's piano instrumentals still find their home.
